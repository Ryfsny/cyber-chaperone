# 3. MOU structure we need now

The MOU must reflect the **current evolved system**, not only the old Ruka brief.

## MOU — key clauses to include

### 1. Parties

```text
This MOU is between eblockwatch / Andre Snyman and Ruka / SmartChat, with Kriszti Newell involved for eblockshop administration and commercial coordination where applicable.
```

### 2. Purpose

```text
The purpose is to support the development, operation, and commercial rollout of AI Arnie, Cyber Chaperone, the eblockwatch WhatsApp member funnel, and associated membership / eblockshop / payment flows.
```

### 3. What eblockwatch owns

```text
eblockwatch owns:
- the eblockwatch brand
- AI Arnie concept and wording
- Cyber Chaperone concept and operating model
- Situation Room model
- member relationships
- member data
- WhatsApp scripts and menu logic
- eblockshop positioning
- commercial funnel
- local conduit network concept
- Bliksim positioning and confidential capabilities
```

### 4. What Ruka / SmartChat provides

```text
Ruka / SmartChat provides:
- WhatsApp communication platform support
- SmartChat / WhatsApp automation where used
- technical implementation support
- message routing support
- integration support
- ongoing platform assistance where agreed
```

### 5. Current architecture reality

```text
The current evolved architecture may use Replit, Twilio, ChatGPT, PostgreSQL, OpenStreetMap/OSRM, and other tools in addition to or instead of Ruka / SmartChat.

Ruka is not the exclusive brain of the system unless separately agreed in writing.
```

### 6. Situation Room privacy

```text
The Situation Room is private and operator-controlled.

Members, ICE contacts, responders, and local conduits do not have direct access to the Situation Room.

Andre / operator controls:
- who is invited into a case
- what information they receive
- when they are removed
- what action is taken
```

### 7. Member data and privacy

```text
Member data belongs to eblockwatch and must not be copied, reused, sold, exported, marketed to, or exposed without written permission from eblockwatch.
```

### 8. No direct member-to-responder lane

```text
Responders / local conduits do not receive direct member access by default.

All communication is mediated by the Situation Room unless Andre/operator approves otherwise.
```

### 9. Commercial model

```text
Revenue layers include:
- Entry / free member funnel
- Single Membership at R150/month
- Family Membership at R250/month
- Bliksim sales / service subscriptions
- eblockshop products
- future value-added safety services
```

### 10. Confidentiality

```text
Bliksim capabilities, Situation Room methods, member data, trip logic, local conduit logic, commercial flows, and technical implementation details are confidential.
```

```text

Member: Andre Snyman
Trip: Fourways → Oyster Box Durban
Reason: ETA drift / missed check-in / unclear movement
Original ETA: 23:30
Current concern: [detail]

Next action: Ask member to confirm status.
```

```text

Member: Andre Snyman
```

# 8. Local Conduit dispatch message

This must be controlled by the Situation Room.

```text
Cyber Chaperone request from the eblockwatch Situation Room.

A member may need assistance near [area/location].

Status: [AMBER / RED]

Are you able to assist or help mobilise trusted local support?

Reply:
1. I can assist directly
2. I can alert my local safety network
3. I can contact a trusted responder
4. I cannot assist now
5. Please ask the Situation Room to call me
```

**Do not include by default:**

```text
member phone number
direct WhatsApp link to member
full trip history
evidence notes
private notes
exact home address
dashboard links
```
